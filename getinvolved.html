Get Involved

EDUCATION

Take the time to browse our website. We have included some great tips on taking the first steps to a "Zero Waste" Lifestyle and links to other useful resources and web sites.

VOLUNTEER

Volunteering is a great way to make friends and meet new people.

START YOUR OWN LOCAL GROUP

Get to know your neighbors. Organize a local committee and work together to clean up your area. Work with local supermarkets and businesses and find out what they're plans are to reduce the use of single use plastics and other unnecessary waste

CONTACT YOUR LOCAL AUTHORITY

Check your local authorities web site for recycling facilities and if there is none, contact them and let them know.
