About Us

I have always thought of myself as an environmentally conscious citizen. I separated my recyclables at home, never littered and always threw my coffee cup in the bin and I assumed that was enough.

Last year I came across a video on Youtube that changed my life.

A team of scientists were out on a study researching Olive Ridley Sea Turtles off the Pacific shore of Costa Rica. The team came across an adult male turtle and brought it onboard when they noticed something stuck in its nose. That something proved to be a 10cm plastic straw.

Since watching that video I have always refused the BIG 4 of Plastic waste:- PLASTIC STRAWS, PLASTIC BOTTLES, PLASTIC BAGS and DISPOSABLE CUPS.

I have now become an advocate of the "Zero Waste" Lifestyle. Recently I contacted my own employer to raise concerns about the amount of disposable cups people were using and discarding and they have now adopted the use of compostable cups and lids aswell as reviewing and updating the company Recycling policy


My aim is to raise awareness about the devastating impact these single use items are having on the streets of Dublin City and educate people on what they can do to help by making small changes to their own lifestyles.
