What is a KeepCup?

A KeepCup is a reusable hot beverage container that is great for holding Tea and Coffee. They come in a wide variety of styles in either Glass or Plastic and are free from BPA. Bisphenol A or BPA is an industrial chemical found in certain plastics and exposure to large quantities can have a serious impact on your body and your health including brain behavior and blood pressure.

One of the many benefits of using a KeepCup are the discounts that most Irish Cafes now give when you bring in your CLEAN KeepCup They are widely available and start at around €8.99. Pick up your KeepCup today.
