Traveling by Car

  1. Follow the signs for Dublin / M1
  2. Continue south onto the M1 Motorway
  3. At M1 junction 1, take the M50 exit to N7/N2/N11/N3/N4/N81
  4. At the roundabout, take the 3rd exit onto M50
  5. At junction 6, take the N3 exit to Blanchardstown
  6. Keep in the left hand lane and you will enter the N3
  7. Take either of the next two exits off the N3 at the Blanchardstown Centre
